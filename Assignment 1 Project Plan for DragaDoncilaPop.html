Goals

This website is intended to increase Slammer's weekly number of younger players.

Success Evaluation

  1. The number of players weekly should be measured, with an increase to ~150 players/wk considered successful
  2. The average age of new players should be calculated, and should decrease in comparison to pre-website average attendee age to be considered successful

Target Audience

Males and females aged 15 to 30 of any fitness level. The site will appeal to the target group by emphasising the social aspect of Slammers Beach Volleyball, as well as the bar facilities and Happy Hour. Site will also feature option to connect with Slammers across social media, making it more likely for the target age group to engage.
